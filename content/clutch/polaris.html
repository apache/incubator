Apache Polaris (Incubating)

Polaris is a catalog for data lakes. It provides new levels of choice, flexibility and control over data, with full enterprise security and Apache Iceberg interoperability across a multitude of engines and infrastructure.

  • Resources: polaris

  • Started: 2024-08-09; Last Status Update: 2024-08-27

  • Reporting: March, June, September, December

  • Committers: 26

  • PPMC members: 17

  • Mentors: Holden Karau (holden), Kent Yao (yao), Ryan Blue (blue), JB Onofre (jbonofre), Francois Papon (fpapon)
