Apache Kyuubi (Incubating)

Kyuubi is a distributed multi-tenant Thrift JDBC/ODBC server for large-scale data management, processing, and analytics, built on top of Apache Spark and designed to support more engines.

  • Resources: kyuubi

  • Started: 2021-06-21; Last Status Update: 2022-09-06

  • Reporting: March, June, September, December

  • Committers: 17

  • PPMC members: 9

  • Mentors: Willem Ning Jiang (ningjiang), Jeff Zhang (zjffdu), Duo Zhang (zhangduo), Akira Ajisaka (aajisaka)
