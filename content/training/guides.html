Guides & Documentation

Key resources to support mentors, podlings, and the Incubator PMC. These guides cover onboarding, mentoring practices, governance, reporting, and lessons learned.

Incubator Guides

These guides provide reference material for mentors, podlings, and IPMC members on every aspect of incubation — from getting started to graduation. They represent the written foundation for the Incubator’s training materials and slide decks.

Getting Started / Orientation

  • Joining the Incubator — what to expect before applying, reasons to join (or not), and how the proposal process works

  • Select Your Mentors — how to build a balanced, independent, and effective mentor team before entering incubation

  • Podling Orientation Guide — introduction to incubation and ASF culture

  • Podling Committer Onboarding — what new committers need to know

  • Podling PPMC Onboarding — duties and decision-making within the PPMC

  • Mentor Onboarding — step-by-step entry into mentoring

  • Incubator PMC Onboarding — orientation for joining the IPMC

  • Mentor Quick Start Guide — fast orientation for new mentors

Communication and Collaboration

  • Communication in Apache Projects — why mailing lists matter, acceptable chat use, and meeting best practices

  • International & Cultural Awareness — working across languages and time zones

Governance and Culture

  • Governance in Practice — applying the Apache Way in daily decisions

  • Why Governance Matters — why ASF governance underpins sustainable, community-led projects

  • Who Does What — responsibilities across Mentors, PPMCs, the IPMC, and ASF bodies

  • IPMC Oversight — how the IPMC provides oversight, guidance, and course correction across podlings

  • Common Myths — clears up frequent misconceptions about incubation purpose, votes, and timelines

  • Vendor Neutrality — maintaining balanced, independent governance

  • Neutrality In Practice — practical guidance on how incubating projects maintain community-led, vendor-neutral governance

  • Voting and Consensus in Practice — understanding consensus, voting types, and how decisions are made

  • Selecting a PMC Chair — how to identify and elect project leadership

Legal, Infrastructure, and Project Lifecycle

  • Third-Party Branding and Incubating Projects — guidance on names, logos, and brand use during incubation

  • Legal Basics — essential ASF legal and licensing principles for podlings

  • Privacy & Data Handling — practical rules for collecting, storing, and sharing data in podlings

  • Interacting with ASF Infrastructure — how projects work effectively with ASF Infra and shared systems

  • Measuring Podling Health — assessing community activity, diversity, and mentor engagement

  • Common Incubation Paths — overview of typical project origins and how communities progress through incubation

  • Graduation Criteria — what’s required for a podling to graduate

  • Graduation and Beyond — post-graduation transition process

  • Retiring Podlings — how and when to retire an inactive podling

Mentoring, Reporting, and Practice

  • Mentor Handbook — consolidated reference for mentors with links to policy and training materials

  • Release Vote Insights — analysis of vote patterns and lessons from past release reviews

  • Best Practices for Mentors — examples and scenarios for effective mentoring

  • Mentors’ Tough Conversation Guide — how to navigate difficult discussions

  • Mentor Offboarding — how mentors transition after graduation

  • Mentor Replacement — guidance on when and how to adjust a podling’s mentor roster

  • Project Report Guide — preparing and submitting ASF project reports

  • How To Use the ASF Maturity Model — tool to assess community readiness and sustainability

  • Incubator Case Studies — lessons learned from past podlings

  • IPMC Reflection and Oversight — lessons and patterns from case studies to strengthen oversight and mentoring practices
