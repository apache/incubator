Column Notes

Date

The date the JIRA issue was created or a thread was started about the improvement.

Name

A descriptive name for the improvement. Should be similar to the original subject whether JIRA or Email.

JIRA Issue

A link to the JIRA issue. https\://issues.apache.org/jira/projects/INCUBATOR/issues/INCUBATOR-231[INCUBATOR-231]

Email Threads

Permalinks to discussions. Please use lists.apache.org. Don’t use nabble or gmane, we have guarantees about apache.org uris and none about third parties. There are a number of ten or more year old discussions that will be difficult to single out due to gmane changes.
