Introduction

These videos introduce key aspects of the Apache Incubator and the broader ASF culture. They complement the slide decks and written guides available on the Incubator and Training websites.

Video Playlist

These videos are part of the Apache Incubator Training Series. They introduce the principles, roles, and practices that guide podlings toward becoming successful Apache projects.

All videos are currently hosted on the ASF YouTube channel.

Orientation and Values

  • Podling Orientation: Starting Your Apache Journey — introduction to the incubation process, what it means to be a podling, and first steps toward becoming an Apache project.

  • ASF Values — overview of the cultural principles that guide all Apache projects: transparency, consensus, meritocracy, and community over code.

Governance and Independence

  • Branding and Identity in the Apache Incubator — guidance on name and logo use, maintaining clear identity, and avoiding brand confusion.

  • Vendor Neutrality in the Apache Incubator — explains why balanced governance is key to project health and independence.

Onboarding and Roles

  • Mentor Onboarding — orientation for new mentors on responsibilities, best practices, and common challenges.

  • IPMC Onboarding — overview of the Incubator PMC’s oversight role and decision-making processes.

  • PPMC Onboarding — guidance for Podling PMCs on governance, leadership, and collaboration.

  • Committer Onboarding — introduction for new committers to rights, responsibilities, and ASF expectations.
